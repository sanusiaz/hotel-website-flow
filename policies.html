Terms and Conditions

Last updated: October 10, 2026

1. Booking and Reservations

Reservations are subject to availability and pricing at the time of booking. A valid credit card is required to secure your reservation. Your card will not be charged until check-out unless otherwise stated in your booking conditions.

2. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 48 hours prior to the arrival date will incur no penalty. Late cancellations or no-shows will be charged the equivalent of the first night's stay.

3. Check-in and Check-out

Check-in time is from 3:00 PM onwards. Check-out time is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out requests are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.
